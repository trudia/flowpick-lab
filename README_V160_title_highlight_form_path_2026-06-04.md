# TrendFlow v160 Title Highlight And Form Path

Date: 2026-06-04

## Summary

- Added soft title highlighting across homepage cards, recent posts, series blocks, and article headings.
- Tuned highlight colors to stay readable without feeling harsh in light and dark mode.
- Changed the homepage gear-diagnosis card to open the related article first.
- Kept the Google Form CTA inside the article body and sidebar so visitors read the context before submitting the form.
- Updated HTML pages to load `styles.css?v=160` so the new highlight style is not blocked by old CSS cache keys.

## Reason

Titles were not standing out enough as the homepage grew. A light marker-style highlight improves scanning while keeping the editorial tone calm. The gear diagnosis flow is also more natural when visitors read the article before moving to the form.
